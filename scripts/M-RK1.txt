M-RK-1: "3 ATTEMPTS FAIL — AB KYA?"
Format: DTC | 6 clips × 8s = 48s + 15s CTA (appended by code) = ~63s
Target: UPSC/SSC exam aspirant, 20-28 male
Setting: His room — books and notes scattered, study table visible, lived-in
Aspect Ratio: 9:16 vertical (Reels / Shorts)
Character: THE GUY — single character throughout, Rishika NEVER on screen

SCRIPT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
 
[CLIP 1 — 8s | HOOK]
Tone: Tired. Direct. Stating fact, not performing pain.
Hook must land in first 3 seconds.
 
"Teen attempt ho gaye. Prelims clear nahi hua.
Gharwale bol rahe hain —
chhod de, ya shaadi kar le."
 
 
[CLIP 2 — 8s | THE SPIRAL]
Tone: Flat, defeated. Brief pause after each "nahi hua."
The rhythm IS the emotion — do not rush.
 
"Pehla — socha hoga. Nahi hua.
Doosra — pakka hoga. Nahi hua.
Teesra — last try. Nahi hua."
 
 
[CLIP 3 — 8s | ISOLATION]
Tone: Quietest clip. This is the bottom.
Phone detail is spoken, not shown — no stage direction, no second action.
 
"Dost settle ho rahe hain, main wahin hoon.
Phone mein koi notification nahi.
Kisi se baat nahi."
 
 
[CLIP 4 — 8s | THE TURN]
Tone: First breath. Not relief yet — the moment just before relief.
"Usne bas suna" is the most important line in the ad.
 
"Raat ko neend nahi aa rahi thi —
SuperLiving pe Rishika se baat ki.
Usne bas suna."
 
 
[CLIP 5 — 8s | RISHIKA'S LINE — QUOTED BY THE GUY]
⚠️ Rishika NEVER appears on screen.
Guy delivers her exact words slowly — like translating something
that didn't fully land when he first heard it. A quiet "oh."
Expression: quietly struck. Not dramatic. Real.
 
"Usne bola — yaar, teen baar fail hona
matlab tu teen baar utha.
Ab chauthi baar uthna hai."
 
 
[CLIP 6 — 8s | PAYOFF]
Tone: Small, grounded confidence. NOT performed happiness.
Same room, same books — but he looks like he belongs there now.
"Khud ko bhi samajh raha hoon" is the line that makes people stop scrolling.
 
"Ab roz baat karta hoon.
Is baar sirf padh nahi raha —
khud ko bhi samajh raha hoon."
